Here's a quick explanation of each section in the report — concepts explained simply, technical terms kept in English:

1. Executive Summary — Pura report ek nazar mein. Snapshot table + verdict, taaki koi bhi 2 minute mein samajh jaaye ki overall picture kya hai.

2. Problem Validation — Kya problem (rat ko medicine na milna) sach mein bada hai? Yahan secondary data (govt reports, health stats) se prove
3.  kiya gaya hai — lekin yeh bata diya gaya ki tumne abhi khud villagers se baat nahi ki, woh gap bachi hai.

4. Founder-Market Fit — Yeh idea banane ke liye tum kitne "fit" ho — tumhari skills (coding), gaps (hardware, regulatory, capital) ko score karke dikhaya gaya, radar chart ke saath.

5. TAM, SAM, SOM — Market size funnel: TAM = whole India OTC market ($8.2B), SAM = jo realistically target kar sakte ho (rural backward states, $1.2B),
6. SOM = jo Year-1 mein actually achieve kar sakte ho as a side project (~$25-40K).

7. Competitor Analysis — Kaun-kaun already is space mein hai (Jan Aushadhi, ASHA workers, Vendekin, WEIMI, etc.) aur unki weaknesses kya hain.

8. Market Gap Analysis — Competitors ke beech mein konsa "white space" khula hai jisko koi fill nahi kar raha — yahan dikhaya gaya ki rural + 24x7 automation ka combo missing hai.

9. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) — Tumhara perfect customer kaun hai — demographics, location, behavior ke terms mein (end-user villager + NGO partner, dono).

10. Buyer Persona — ICP ka ek real insaan jaisa version — naam, story, trigger moment ke saath ("Ramesh", "Sunita") — taaki tum unki nazar se sochlo.

11. Customer Pain Points — Unki exact problems, severity aur frequency ke saath ranked.

12. Buying Triggers & Objections — Kya unhe kharidne ke liye push karega (trigger) vs kya unhe hesitate karayega (objection).

13. Customer Journey — Awareness se lekar repeat-use tak ka path — har stage pe drop-off ka risk kya hai.

14. Risk Assessment — Sab kuch jo galat ho sakta hai — likelihood, impact, aur uska mitigation.

15. Pivot Opportunities — Agar original (hardware vending machine) idea risky lage, to safer/lighter alternatives kya ho sakte hain (jaise WhatsApp bot).

16. Go / No-Go Recommendation — Final verdict — build karna chahiye ya nahi, aur kis form mein.

17. 30-Day Action Plan — Next 30 din mein exactly kya karna hai, week-by-week.

18. Appendix — Sources list + disclaimer (yeh legal/medical advice nahi hai).
