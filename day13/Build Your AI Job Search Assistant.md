Today's Topic- "Build Your AI Job Search Assistant"

In current Job market, it is very difficult fi find best jobs that match your skills and also paying at reasonable price. 
With Claude AI you can search through Platforms with just 3 prompts that will give you the best results according to your skills and Demands.

PROMPT 1: Professional Profile

Describe your professional background, including:

Current role
Years of experience
Key skills and technologies
Industry/domain expertise
Current company type
Current location
Notable achievements, certifications, or accomplishments

PROMPT 2: Job Search Criteria

Specify your target job requirements, including:

Desired job titles
Preferred company types
Preferred locations (Remote/Hybrid/Onsite)
Salary expectations
Industries or companies to exclude
Job posting recency requirements
Any additional preferences or constraints

PROMPT 3: Job Discovery & Analysis

Using my professional profile and job search criteria:

Search for matching job opportunities using the available job connector(s)

Exclude jobs that do not meet my requirements

Company
Role

Match Score
Why It Fits My Profile

Most commonly required skills across the jobs

Market demand insights

Results-------------------------------------------
Most commonly required skills across postings: JavaScript and Git/GitHub appeared in nearly every listing, 
followed closely by React/Node.js and REST API experience. SQL or NoSQL database knowledge (PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB)
showed up in most roles too. TypeScript and cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP) appeared in a smaller but still notable share,
usually in the higher-paying postings.
Skill gap analysis: Your listed skills (JS, HTML, CSS, Python, communication) cover the fundamentals almost every 
posting expects, but the gap is in applied tooling: no framework experience (React, Node/Express, or Django/Flask) shows 
up in your profile, no Git/version-control mention, and no database experience. These three gaps, more than salary, are 
what's keeping you out of the stronger matches above (PS Technology and Vrhunec IT score highest partly because their core
ask overlaps your raw skills, but even those want some hands-on framework or years of experience you haven't indicated having).
Market demand insights: In the current remote/hybrid Indian job market, ₹50k–80k/month roles are concentrated among 
candidates with at least one production-grade framework (commonly MERN-stack: MongoDB, Express, React, Node) plus 1–3 years of
experience or a strong specialized stack (AI tooling, TypeScript, SaaS architecture). Pure fundamentals-only profiles, like yours appears to be on paper, are mostly being matched to internships paying ₹0–15k/month stipends rather than full-time roles at your target band.
Recommendations to improve interview chances:

Build 2–3 portfolio projects in a framework (React + Node, or Python with Flask/Django) and host the code on GitHub — this single
addition would meaningfully raise your match score across nearly every listing above.
Add a database to your toolkit (MySQL or MongoDB) since most postings assume some familiarity.
Lead with your JS certification and UBA coordinator role in applications since they signal initiative and communication strength,
which several postings explicitly value, but pair them with the technical additions above rather than relying on them alone.
Consider a short-term internship in the ₹10k–30k/month range (like MARK1 or Salax) to build a documented "experience" line, then
re-target ₹50k+ roles in 6–12 months once you have that plus a framework under your belt.

Overall fit assessment: Your profile is currently best aligned with entry-level/internship listings rather than the ₹50k–80k/month 
full-time roles you're targeting. The compensation goal is realistic but more likely a 6–12 month target after closing the
framework/Git/database gap, with PS Technology and Vrhunec IT being your closest near-term shots given their lighter experience bar relative to the rest of the pool.
